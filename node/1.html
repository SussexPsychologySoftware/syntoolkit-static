We are the Multisense Synaesthesia Research lab, directed by Prof. Julia Simner at the University of Sussex, and funded by the European Research Council. Our aim is to promote a clear scientific understanding of synaesthesia.

This website provide FAQs about synaesthesia, and a range of information for different users. If you know a child with synaesthesia, our website provides information for parents and educators about synaesthesia in the classroom. If you are a researcher, our website provides a library of tests for your research. If you are an educational psychologist or other clinician, our website will soon provide a library of tests to diagnose synaesthesia in your child or adult clients.
